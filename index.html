Summary: Latitude vs. X

How does the weather change the closer you venture to the equator? In this project, I analyze this question by pulling data from the OpenWeatherMap API and assembling a dataset on over 500 cities.

Using Matplotlib, I plotted various aspects of the weather vs, latitude, looking at temperature, cloudiness, wind speed, and humidity.

Within this site you will find detailed analysis of any noticeable trends, correlations, and explainations, accompanied by visuals, and source data.
